POTASSIUM NITRATE

【Character & Purpose】

Potassium nitrate is the raw material for making black gunpowder, such as mine gunpowder, fuses, firecrackers, etc. . Also used in fireworks to produce purple sparks. A salt bath for quenching by mechanical heat treatment. The ceramic industry is used to make ceramic glazes and paints. Used as a glass clarifier. The utility model is used for manufacturing automobile lamp glass shell, optical glass picture tube glass shell, etc. . Pharmaceutical industry for the production of penicillin potassium salt, rifampicin and other drugs. Used for making cigarette paper. Used as a catalyst. Dressing agent. Used as a compound fertilizer for crops and flowers. Potassium nitrate is also a permitted hair color in China. It is reduced to potassium nitrite due to bacterial action in meat products and acts as a colour-protecting and antimicrobial agent. China stipulates that it can be used in meat products, with a maximum consumption of 0.5 g/kg and a residue of 0.03 g/kg. Industrial potassium nitrate is also widely used in glass strengthening process.
