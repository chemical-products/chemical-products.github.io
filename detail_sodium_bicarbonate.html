SODIUM BICARBONATE

【Character & Purpose】

Sodium bicarbonate can be directly used as a raw material in the pharmaceutical industry to treat excessive stomach acid. It can also be used in film production, tanning, mineral processing, smelting, metal heat treatment, as well as in the fiber and rubber industries. It is also used as a detergent for wool and for soaking seeds in agriculture. One of the most widely used loosening agents in the food industry, it is used in the production of biscuits, cakes, Mantou, bread, etc. It is a carbon dioxide generator in soft drinks;
