About Us

River and Mountain Global Limited, is abbreviated as 'RMG', a company with more than 15 years experience in chemical industry. With our extensive industry experience, robust business strategies, and keen market insights, we have steadily established our position in the competitive chemical products market and gradually established our own industry status.

We adhere to the core concept of the most competitive price , the best service, the Reliable source of products, and take customer satisfaction as the highest standard to measure our work.

We specialize in the export trade of chemical products in industries such as glassware, printing and dyeing, water treatment, detergents, food and feed additives, fertilizers, desiccants, plastic products, mineral products, paper-making, and foaming agents. All of our products originate from national-level enterprises with a stable product quality and supply guarantee.
