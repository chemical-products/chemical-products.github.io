Basic information and industry applications of PET POLYETHYLENE GLYCOL TEREPHTHALATE



1、 Basic information

PET POLYETHYLENE GLYCOL TEREPHTHALATE (abbreviated as PET) is a polymer ester formed by the combination of ethylene glycol and terephthalic acid, with the chemical formula of Polyethylene terephthalate. PET, as a high-performance thermoplastic, is widely used in multiple industries due to its excellent physical and chemical properties

2、 Industry use


2.1 Packaging industry

The application of PET in the packaging industry is particularly prominent. As early as the mid-1970s, PET containers were widely used in the soft drink industry, such as soda and fruit juice. In recent years, PET containers have gradually entered the field of beer packaging, and are favored by consumers due to their high transparency, impact resistance, and resistance to breakage. Meanwhile, PET is also used as packaging for cosmetics, seasonings, edible oils, and other products

2.2 Construction industry

In the construction industry, PET board is widely loved for its beauty, elegance, and texture, and is an excellent building material for furniture such as cabinets and wardrobes. The advantage of PET board lies not only in its appearance, but also in its good weather resistance, corrosion resistance, and ease of processing

2.3 Clothing industry

PET also has a wide range of applications in the clothing industry. The production technology of ultra-fine PET fibers is constantly improving, making the application of PET fibers in the clothing field increasingly widespread. Through specific processing techniques, PET fibers can be made into various high-performance clothing fabrics, such as waterproof, windproof, breathable, etc

2.4 Electronics, Electrical and Automotive Industry

In the electronic, electrical, and automotive industries, PET is used for various coil skeletons, transformers, televisions, recorder components and casings, automotive lamp holders, lampshades, incandescent lamp holders, relays, selenium rectifiers, and more. The excellent electrical and mechanical properties of PET make it an important material in these industries

2.5 Environmental protection field

In recent years, with the increasing awareness of environmental protection, the environmental application of PET has also received much attention. Research has shown that certain bacteria can decompose PET plastics, providing new ideas for the environmentally friendly treatment of PET. In the future, with the continuous advancement of technology, the application of PET in the field of environmental protection will be more extensive

3、 Summary

PET POLYETHYLENE GLYCOL TEREPHTHALATE, as a high-performance thermoplastic, has a wide range of applications in various industries such as packaging, construction, clothing, electronics and electronics, and automobiles. With the continuous advancement of technology and the improvement of environmental awareness, the application prospects of PET will be even broader
