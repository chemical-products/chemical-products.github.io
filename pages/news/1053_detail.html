Introduction and Industry Use of TDI Toluene Diisocyanate

1、 Introduction

TDI (toluene diisocyanate) is an organic compound with the chemical formula C9H6N2O2. It is a colorless or slightly yellow transparent liquid with a strong irritating odor. TDI is mainly used to produce polyurethane (PU) materials, such as polyurethane foam, polyurethane elastomer, polyurethane coatings and adhesives

TDI molecules contain two isocyanate groups (- NCO), which can react with compounds containing active hydrogen (such as polyols, amines, etc.) to form polyurethane. Polyurethane is an important polymer material with excellent physical and chemical properties, such as wear resistance, oil resistance, solvent resistance, aging resistance, etc

2、 Industry use

2.1 Polyurethane foam

TDI is one of the main raw materials for the production of polyurethane foam. Polyurethane foam has excellent thermal insulation, heat insulation and sound insulation properties, and is widely used in construction, automobile, household appliances and other fields. In the construction field, polyurethane foam is often used as wall insulation material, roof insulation material and floor insulation material

2.2 Polyurethane elastomer

TDI is also used to produce polyurethane elastomers, which are materials with excellent elasticity and wear resistance. Polyurethane elastomers are widely used in fields such as shoe soles, tires, conveyor belts, and seals. In shoe sole manufacturing, polyurethane elastomers can provide a soft and comfortable foot feel and good wear resistance

2.3 Polyurethane coating

TDI is also used in the production of polyurethane coatings. Polyurethane coatings have excellent weather resistance, corrosion resistance, wear resistance, and decorative properties, and are widely used in fields such as automobiles, furniture, and architecture. In the automotive industry, polyurethane coatings are often used as coatings on body paint surfaces, interior components, and other areas

2.4 Adhesive

TDI is also widely used in the field of adhesives. Polyurethane adhesive has excellent bonding strength, water resistance, and chemical corrosion resistance, and can be used for bonding various materials such as metals, plastics, rubber, wood, etc

2.5 Other fields

In addition to the above-mentioned fields, TDI can also be used to produce some special chemicals, such as polyurethane fibers (spandex), polyurethane sealants, etc

3、 Summary

TDI toluene diisocyanate, as an important chemical raw material, plays an important role in the production of polyurethane materials. Its wide range of applications and excellent performance make TDI play an important role in the chemical industry
